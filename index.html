Welcome...

William J. Arendt & Associates, P.C. is a full-service law firm, concentrating in family and business law matters. Conveniently located in Burr Ridge, Illinois, we serve clients in the Chicagoland area in the state courts, at all levels of the federal court system, including the United States District Court for the Northern District of Illinois, the United States Court of Appeals for the Seventh Circuit, and the United States Supreme Court, as well as in the Equal Employment Opportunity Commission and the Illinois Department of Human Rights.

Our extensive courtroom experience, work ethic, and proven track record of success separates us from the competition. We have earned the respect of our clients, judges, and opposing attorneys. We are knowledgeable, diligent, innovative, and aggressive. Our attorneys provide quality, cost-effective legal services with a high degree of personal attention. We are results-oriented, focusing on developing and implementing successful and time-efficient legal strategies. We are dedicated to the traditional values of integrity, perseverance, and legal excellence while employing modern technology to deliver prompt, professional, positive, and cost-effective legal services of the highest quality.

Family Law. We have extensive and encompassing experience in all aspects of family law, ranging from divorce and child custody to complex business and financial evaluations and property division. Our attorneys provide the guidance and advocacy needed during even in the most difficult family law tribulations. For additional information on our family law practice, see the Family Law section of our website, or contact us directly.

Business Law. Whether you own a small business or represent a larger organization, William J. Arendt & Associates, P.C. can help you enforce your rights and protect your interests. We have experience in a wide variety of business matters, including general counsel services, litigation services involving fraud, breach of contract, landlord and tenant, employment disputes, transactional and banking matters, and more. For additional information on our business law practice, see the business law section of our website, or contact us directly.
